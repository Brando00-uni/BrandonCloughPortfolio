Hi, I'm Brandon

I am a 26 year old undergraduate student studying Game Development at the University centre Leeds!

Before tackling game development, I studied fabrication and welding for 3 years and have 2 years of job experience in the field. I always had a passion for games and wanted to learn how to make them, so I decided to go back to university and pursue a career in game development. I am currently in my second year of study and have a strong interest in programming. I am excited to continue learning and developing my skills in the field of game development!

I have experience with C++ and C# programming languages, as well as game engines such as and Unreal Engine. I have also worked on several game projects, both independently and as part of a team, which has allowed me to develop my skills in game design, programming, and project management. I am always eager to learn new technologies and techniques in the field of game development, and I am committed to creating engaging and innovative games that provide players with memorable experiences.
