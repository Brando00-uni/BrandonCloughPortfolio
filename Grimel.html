Grimel

A 3D horror game built around atmosphere, page collection, and a narrative mystery.

About

Grimel is a narrative-driven 3D horror game developed in Unreal Engine. The player collects pages and uncovers the story of Grimel while moving through an immersive environment.

Project Info

Role
Gameplay Programmer
Team Size
1
Time Frame
3 months
Engine
Unreal Engine Blueprints

Introduction

Grimel was the first project I worked on during my first year of university. It was a solo project developed in Unreal Engine using Blueprints, with the goal of learning the basics of Unreal Engine while producing a small but polished horror experience.

What I Learned

Through Grimel, I learned the basics of Unreal Engine and Blueprint scripting. I also explored how lighting, sound, pacing, and level layout can support atmosphere and immersion in a narrative-driven game.
