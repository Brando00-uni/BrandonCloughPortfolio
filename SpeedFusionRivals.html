Speed Fusion Rivals

A fast-paced racing game focused on speed, handling, and readable moment-to-moment gameplay.

About

Speed Fusion Rivals is a racing game developed in Unreal Engine using Blueprints. It focuses on intense racing action with an emphasis on speed, skill, and strategy.

Introduction

Speed Fusion Rivals was the first solo project I worked on as I began my game development journey. I developed it in Unreal Engine using Blueprints, focusing on learning engine fundamentals while creating a small racing experience.

What I Learned

Through Speed Fusion Rivals, I learned the basics of Unreal Engine and Blueprint scripting. I also explored how movement, vehicle feel, level layout, and player feedback affect the pace and challenge of a racing game.
