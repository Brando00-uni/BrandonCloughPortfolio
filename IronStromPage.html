Iron Storm

About

Iron Storm is a 3D First Person Shooter game set within a trench warefare enviorment.

Project Info

  • 👤 Role: Lead Programmer
  • 👥 Team Size: 4
  • 🕒 Time frame: 3 months
  • 🛠 Engine: Unreal Engine (Blueprints)

Introduction

Iron Storm was the second group project I worked on during my first year of university, where I was the lead programmer. It was a group project developed in Unreal Engine using Blueprints.

What I Had Learned

During the development of Iron Storm, I had the opportunity to learn and implement various gameplay mechanics using Unreal Engine's Blueprint visual scripting system. I was responsible for creating the core gameplay systems, including player movement, shooting mechanics and AI. Through this project, I gained valuable experience in game development, teamwork, and problem-solving, which has helped me grow as a game developer working within smaller teams.
