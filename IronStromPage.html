Introduction

Iron Storm was the second group project I worked on during my first year of university. The game was developed in Unreal Engine using Blueprints, and I was responsible for leading gameplay programming work across core systems.

What I Learned

During Iron Storm, I implemented player movement, shooting mechanics, and AI behaviour using Unreal Engine's Blueprint visual scripting system. The project helped me improve my teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills in a small production environment.
